How to submit your entry

Submit your entry by emailing engines () bunnyzone ! org. Here's what to include:

  • Source code: either a tarball (compressed or uncompressed), zip file, or link (Github, Gitlab, Sourcehut, &c).
  • Build instructions including compiler versions and dependencies.
  • Your name and the name of your program. (optional — but don't omit both!)
  • A square image that looks good at all scales to represent your entry. (optional)
  • An email address to contact you if you win or your program needs revision. (optional — if omitted, I'll reply directly to the email you sent)

If you win, you'll be asked for:

  • Your t-shirt size.
  • Your mailing address for your prizes (or a SkyTrain station you'd like to meet at to pick them up).
